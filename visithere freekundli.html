How to Read Kundli

Kundli Reading

Kundli making and Kundli reading in India is one of the ancient practices used for predicting one's future and discovery of life events. Also called a birth chart or horoscope, a Kundli entails the karmic map of the person’s past, present and future events, pictured after learning the movement of planets, constellations and much more.

It is a belief that once a person has the chart of his life prepared, it can help him in making better choices in life. Apart from this, your Kundli can also give you a picture of your mental, emotional, physical and spiritual state of mind and how it would pan out as you grow.

Significance and Importance of Kundli Reading

The significance of Kundli can be asserted from the fact that it is one of the first things that most Indian parents consider preparing for their child soon after his/her birth. A person’s birth chart is prepared after taking into account their date of birth, place of birth, and time of birth.

Based on this information, an astrologer can find the astrological position of different planets in the different houses of the Kundli at the time of the child's birth. These positions not only define the person's character but also help in understanding the effect of various planets on different aspects of life, such as love, marriage, education, and career.

What Can Kundli and Kundli Reading Help You With?

  • Finding doshas and yogas in your chart, such as Manglik dosha or Gajakesari yoga.
  • Matching the Kundli of a girl and boy before fixing their marriage.
  • Predicting the challenges and opportunities in your future.
  • Helping in better decision-making for yourself and loved ones.
  • Determining the best career path based on your efforts and intellect.
  • Finding solutions for problems like job delays or marriage delays.

FAQ - How to Read Kundli

Can Kundli predict the future correctly?

Yes, Kundli reading can help forecast the future accurately with the help of birth details.

What is a Lagna chart?

A Lagna chart, also known as a Rashi chart, reveals the ascendant of the native and helps understand planetary transit.

What is the Navamsa chart?

The Navamsa chart divides zodiac signs into nine sub-divisions and plays a significant role in Vedic astrology.

What can you expect during an astrology reading session?

An astrologer will analyze your Kundli, describe your inner resources, and reveal the purpose of your soul.
